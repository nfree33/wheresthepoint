How to Play

'Where's the Point?' is based off a 2019 viral game that took the nation by storm. It's fun, easy, and can be played anywhere!

The rules are simple. Players take alternating turns on offense and defense:

  • Offense: you point either up, down, left, or right, using the directionals on the keypad.
  • Defense: With no hesitation, you must look in a different direction from the offensive players 'point'.
  • In this digital version, you have three lives. The game is over once you or the CPU loses all their lives.
